    Welcome to the Automated Lecturers' Time-table Generator web platform. This is a web-based platform which mainly allows you to generate time-tables for the lecturers instantly without wasting time. You also allowed to view and download generated time-tables. You can find the options in the left side panel for manage users, subjects, lecture halls and also customize your profile info.

    NOTE: Currently this is available for Department of Computing & Information Systems only.
